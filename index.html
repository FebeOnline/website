About Us

febe.online is a software services company operating in the Netherlands and South Africa. We aim to deliver the best services to our customers whilst stimulating economic development and innovation in South Africa. We achieve this by training and including young African developers in our various business projects. By bringing your business to febe, you not only benefit from excelent service and great products, but you simultanuously help stimulate the tech industry in Africa.
